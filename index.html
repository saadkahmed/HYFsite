What is Hack Your Future?


HackYourFuture Canada is a coding academy adapted by Refugee Career Jumpstart project (RCJP) [hyperlink to RCJP website] that teaches refugees with no programming/coding background essential skills to start a career in the technical field. With the expansion of the IT bubble, these skills have now become more in demand.

3-hour classes are held once a week during the evening. We also offer extra help, or tutoring sessions during the weekends. Upon completion of the program, Resume/Cover Letter/LinkedIn/Interview workshops are held to prepare the students for the job market. Once ready, we assist our students with finding employment with our network of employers.

Who Can Apply?


Talented Canadian Refugees who want to start their career in web-development. You must be motivated and be willing it to put in a lot of effort and time to become great at coding. You will need to have an intermediate level in English with basic computer skills.
