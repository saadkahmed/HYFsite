What is Hack Your Future?


HackYourFuture Canada is a coding academy adapted by Refugee Career Jumpstart project (RCJP) that teaches refugees the essential skills to start a career in the technical field. With the expansion of the IT bubble, these skills have increased in demand.

3-hour classes are held once a week during the evening. We also offer extra help, or tutoring sessions during the weekends. Upon completion of the program, Resume/Cover Letter/LinkedIn/Interview workshops are held to prepare the students for the job market. Once ready, we assist our students with finding employment through our network of employers.
